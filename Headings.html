The Best Movies According to Justin

My top 3 movies of All Time.

Avengers Endgame

Exciting, entertaining, and emotionally impactful, Avengers: Endgame does whatever it takes to deliver a satisfying finale to Marvel's epic Infinity Saga.

Avatar

The film's most notable feature is its cutting-edge CGI, creating a breathtaking and detailed alien landscape on Pandora with diverse flora and fauna, making it feel incredibly real.

One Piece Red Film

Stunning animation quality, captivating musical numbers featuring J-Pop star Ado, well-developed character arcs, particularly around Uta's story, and its unique approach to incorporating a musical style into the One Piece universe.
